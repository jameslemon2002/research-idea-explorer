# Shared schema

## Fields

- `Object`
  What is being studied.
- `Puzzle`
  What is unresolved in current knowledge.
- `Claim`
  What kind of knowledge the study aims to produce.
- `Contrast`
  What difference makes the claim testable.
- `Evidence`
  What material could adjudicate the claim.
- `Scope`
  For whom, where, when, and at what scale the claim should hold.
- `Stakes`
  Why the result matters.

## Red-team prompts

- Is this just a well-known problem with fresher wording?
- If the chosen method disappears, does the research question still stand?
- What is the smallest runnable study design?
- Which nearby literature would make this look derivative?
